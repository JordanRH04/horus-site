Setup guide

From zero to a farming loop in about two minutes. No Python, no account needed for the free tier.

1

Download & unzip

Grab the free Horus.zip from the Discord and unzip the whole folder anywhere.

2

Run Horus.exe

Double-click Horus.exe. No install, no Python — the app opens straight away.

3

Open the game

Launch Anime Expeditions in windowed mode and get to a stage you want to farm. Horus sizes the window to one standard automatically — you'll see it snap into place with the Horus frame around it. That's what makes one recording work on every PC.

4

Record once

On the RECORD tab, name it, hit Start Recording, and play your setup. Press F7 to stop — it saves a layout.

5

Play it back

Switch to PLAY, pick your layout, press START (F8). It runs the stage exactly as you did.

6

Go unattended (Premium)

Subscribe, then paste the key from your Discord DM into Horus once — it saves. Horus attaches from the Victory/Defeat screen (or mid-wave: open Settings and it taps Restart for you) and loops forever, claiming rewards between runs.

It's not clicking the buttons? Hit “Fix detection” on the screen with the problem button — the Victory/Defeat screen, the Start Game vote screen, or mid-battle — and it re-learns those buttons from your exact screen. Run the game windowed (Horus can't auto-size a fullscreen game) at minimum graphics on weaker PCs.
Weak or laggy PC? Recognition needs the game to render smoothly — a very old or heavily lagging machine can drop frames faster than any screen-reader can follow. The free version uses the same detection as Premium, so run it first to confirm Horus keeps up on your hardware before subscribing. Full help lives in #support.
