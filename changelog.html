Changelog

What's shipped. Templates get re-cut whenever the game patches its UI.

v1.3.4 Jul 2026 LATEST
One-click “Fix detection” for any PC
  • New “Fix detection” button: on the Victory/Defeat screen, one click re-learns the buttons from your exact display — the reliable fallback for any resolution or graphics setup.
v1.3.3 Jul 2026
Works across PCs — high & low graphics
  • Detection now recognizes buttons on both high- and low-graphics setups, so the loop runs the same whether your PC is a beast or a potato.
  • Wider resolution range: the same setup matches across 1080p, 1440p, and 4K without re-capturing anything.
  • HD interface starts more reliably on PCs that have both .NET versions installed.
v1.3.2 Jul 2026
Rock-solid detection & a cleaner, safer loop
  • Re-cut every loop button from the real game UI — Repeat, Restart, and the Game-Started banner now match reliably on a fresh PC.
  • No more mis-fires: if a run hasn’t actually started, Horus waits and retries instead of placing units on the wrong screen.
  • Smarter auto-tuning that only ever adjusts to the correct on-screen banner, and repairs itself if it ever gets it wrong.
v1.3.1 Jul 2026
Loop reliability: cleaner Repeat & Restart detection
  • Re-cut the Victory/Defeat “Repeat Stage” button so the loop reliably restarts instead of stalling on the end screen.
  • Manual restarts now auto-confirm: open Settings → Restart mid-run and Horus taps “Restart” for you, then re-attaches on the next run.
v1.3 Jul 2026
Restart-attach, in-app activation & Discord self-service
  • Restart-attach: start a farm from any point — open Settings mid-wave and Horus taps Restart, confirms, and re-runs your setup the moment “Game Started!” appears.
  • Activate Premium in-app: paste your key once and it sticks across launches; sign out to move machines.
  • Discord self-service: /key re-sends your key, /hwidreset moves your license to a new PC, /status shows your subscription.
  • Instant launch: license checks moved off the startup thread, plus a startup watchdog so the window can never hang.
v1.2 Jul 2026
Reward click-through + theme picker
  • Auto-clicks the rewards screen and secret-unit cutscene between runs.
  • New blue / red / teal UI themes with a live switcher.
  • Faster attach from the Victory/Defeat screen.
v1.1 Jun 2026
Auto-attach & structured layouts
  • Auto-attach: re-queues the next run with no input.
  • Structured layout format for complex raids.
  • DPI-aware capture for high-res displays.
v1.0 May 2026
First public build
  • Record-and-replay core: capture a setup, play it back.
  • Free one-tap replay; Premium auto-loop.
  • Color-coded activity log.
