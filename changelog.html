Changelog

What's shipped. Templates get re-cut whenever the game patches its UI.

v1.3 Jul 2026 LATEST
Restart-attach, in-app activation & Discord self-service
  • Restart-attach: start a farm from any point — open Settings mid-wave and Horus taps Restart, confirms, and re-runs your setup the moment “Game Started!” appears.
  • Activate Premium in-app: paste your key once and it sticks across launches; sign out to move machines.
  • Discord self-service: /key re-sends your key, /hwidreset moves your license to a new PC, /status shows your subscription.
  • Instant launch: license checks moved off the startup thread, plus a startup watchdog so the window can never hang.
v1.2 Jul 2026
Reward click-through + theme picker
  • Auto-clicks the rewards screen and secret-unit cutscene between runs.
  • New blue / red / teal UI themes with a live switcher.
  • Faster attach from the Victory/Defeat screen.
v1.1 Jun 2026
Auto-attach & structured layouts
  • Auto-attach: re-queues the next run with no input.
  • Structured layout format for complex raids.
  • DPI-aware capture for high-res displays.
v1.0 May 2026
First public build
  • Record-and-replay core: capture a setup, play it back.
  • Free one-tap replay; Premium auto-loop.
  • Color-coded activity log.
