Why Horus

Built to set and forget

Think TinyTask, refined. Instead of blindly replaying clicks at fixed coordinates, Horus uses image recognition to read the game state on screen and act on what it actually sees — so it self-corrects, waits for the right moment, and keeps going when a dumb recorder would fall out of sync.

Record & replay

Play a setup once; Horus captures every placement and click as a clean layout file.

Attach from anywhere

Detects the Victory/Defeat screen and re-queues the next run — or takes over mid-wave: open Settings and Horus taps Restart, confirms, and re-runs your setup the moment “Game Started!” appears.

The farm loop

Premium never stops — attach, run, claim rewards, click through cutscenes, repeat. Wake up to a pile of loot.

Structured layouts

Reward click-through

Reads the game, not coordinates
